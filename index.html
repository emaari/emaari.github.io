Platinum Day Date

The Rolex Day-Date 40 reference 228349RBR elevates luxury to celestial heights with its mesmerizing meteorite dial. Crafted from a fragment of the Gibeon meteorite, which fell in Namibia over 4 billion years ago, each dial is a unique masterpiece of natural history and horological artistry. The Gibeon meteorite is renowned for its distinctive Widmanstätten patterns—cross-hatched crystalline structures that formed over millions of years as the meteorite slowly cooled in space. This natural pattern, etched into the dial, gives each timepiece a singular appearance, ensuring that no two dials are alike.
